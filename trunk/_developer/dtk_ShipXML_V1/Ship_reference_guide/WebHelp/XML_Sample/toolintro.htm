Introduction to Shipping

Integrating the Shipping tool into your company's e-commerce solution will enable your customers to access many UPS services, when shipping from 35 origin countries to destinations worldwide. Whether it's creating greater efficiencies in the mailroom or lowering overall costs due to better controls over shipping activities, this UPS OnLine® Tool can streamline your shipping without the addition of expensive hardware or software.
